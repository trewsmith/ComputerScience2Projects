Top Universities to Study Computer Science

US News Rankings

Although the ranking system isn't perfect and every student's needs/wants differ, US News gives us a list of the top colleges for studying Computer Science. Here are the top 5 schools:

  1. Carnegie Mellon University
  2. Massachusetts Institute of Technology
  3. Stanford University
  4. University of California--Berkeley
  5. University of Illinois--Urbana-Champaign

1. Carnegie Mellon University

Some facts about CMU:

  • It has a 7% acceptance rate for the School of Computer Science.
  • It's located in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.
  • It has a total undergraduate enrollment of around 7,500 students.
